Space-r

Instructions

Goal: Move upward to gain points, the further you go the more each level* is worth. Avoid being hit by the flying cats and the asteroids as that will cause your score, round and levels climbed to reset

Controls:

  • Arrow keys: Movement
  • Space: Press Play

Rules: Moving backward provides a small penalty(so that points can't be gained by moving up and down rapidly) Flashing Warning signs indicate an incoming asteroid(approximately 3 seconds)
